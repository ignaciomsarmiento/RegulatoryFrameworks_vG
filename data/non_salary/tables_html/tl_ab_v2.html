Annual & periodic bonuses (Excel look)

Country Annual and other periodic bonuses Legislation
Argentina Annual bonus: 1 monthly wage. Law 23041 (1983).
Bolivia Christmas bonus: 1 monthly wage.

Employment tenure bonus "Antiguedad": 3 MW multiplied by a factor that varies with length of tenure:
- 2-4 years: 5%.
- 5-7 years: 11%.
- 8-10 years: 18%.
- 11-14 years: 26%.
- 15-19 years: 34%.
- 20-24 years: 42%.
- 25 or more years: 50%.

Quinquennial bonus: Employees who have been in service for at least 5 years are entitled to five monthly wages paid every five years.

Economic growth bonus "Doble aguinaldo": 1 monthly wage awarded when the annual June-to-June GDP growth exceeds 4.5%.

Only employees working within 50km of international borders: Border subsidy: 20% of wages.		 Law of December 18th (1944) - Christmas bonus.
Supreme Decree 21137 (1985) - Bono antiguedad and border subsidy.
Supreme Decree 522 (2010) - Bono quinquenio.
Supreme Decree 1802 (2013) - Doble aguinaldo.
Brazil Annual bonus: 1 wage.

Vacation bonus: equivalent to 1/3 wages.		 Decree Law 5.452 (1943) - Vacation bonus.
Law 4090 (1962) - Annual bonus.
Chile No mandatory bonuses.  
Colombia Workers with wages under 13MW: 1 wage. Law 1788 (2016).
Dominican Republic Christmas bonus:
- Workers earning up to 5 MW: 1 wage.
- Workers earning more than 5 MW: 5 MW.		 Law 16 (1992).
Ecuador Christmas bonus: 1 wage.

Annual bonus "Decimocuarta remuneracion": 1 wage.		 Labor Code (2012).
Honduras Annual bonus "Decimo tercer mes": 1 wage. Decree No. 112. (1982) - Ley del Séptimo Día y Décimo Tercer Mes en Concepto de Aguinaldo.
Mexico Annual bonus "Aguinaldo": 0.5 wages.

Vacation bonus: bonus equivalent to 25% of the wages corresponding to their vacation period.		 Federal Labor Law (1970, last reformed in 2023).
Paraguay Annual bonus "aguinaldo": 1 wage. Law No. 213 (2014).
Peru Annual bonus: Workers in medium and large businesses: 2 wages + corresponding employer health contribution (9% or 6.75%, depending on modality), annual.

Family bonus: a monthly bonus equivalent to 10% of the MW for workers who have children under 18 or children under 24 attending tertiary education. This bonus is per worker, not per child.

Workers in small and micro businesses: 1 wage + corresponding employer health contribution (9% or 6.75%, depending on modality), annual.		 Law No. 25129 (1989) - Family Bonus.
Law No. 27735 (2002) - Annual Bonus.
Supreme Decree 013-2013-PRODUCE (2013) - Bonus for micro and small businesses.
United States No mandatory bonuses at the Federal or State level.  
Spain Two extra bonuses are usually paid in June and December, with the option for distribution throughout the year as specified in the contract. Each bonus equals one month's salary. Workers’ Statute (2015, last reformed in 2024).
