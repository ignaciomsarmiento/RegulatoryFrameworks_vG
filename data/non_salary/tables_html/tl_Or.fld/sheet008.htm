Argentina Salaried employees: Insurance premium is made up of a fixed sum per insured employee and a variable rate over the payroll, negotiated between insurance companies and employers. In June 2023, the fixed component was valued at ARS 262 (0.003 MW). At an aggregate level, variable premiums were equivalent to 2.53% of payroll in June 2023.

Bolivia Salaried employees: 1.71%.

Self-employed workers: Workers under contract pay the same rate of 1.71%. Other self-employed are allowed to make voluntary contributions.		 Paid by employer.   Law 065 (2010).
